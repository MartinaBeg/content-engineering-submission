### FAQ *(funnel: objections + SEO)*

**Q: How is Zuora AI different from Salesforce Agentforce or Microsoft Copilot for Finance?**
Horizontal copilots were built for sales, customer service, or general productivity first — finance use cases came after. Zuora AI was built finance-first, inside the system of record. The ASC 606 logic, SSP math, and revenue rules are encoded as product behavior, not learned from training data.

**Q: Does Zuora AI act autonomously, or require human approval?**
It proposes; finance approves. Every recommendation runs through your existing role permissions and approval policies. Simulation is the default mode; posting is the opt-in step.

**Q: Does Zuora AI move sensitive financial data outside the system of record?**
No. Zuora AI reasons in place — inside the same modules finance teams already use. There is no separate AI database, no shadow audit trail, and no second governance layer.

**Q: How does Zuora AI hold up in an audit?**
Every output exposes the actual math — SSP, ASC 606 formulas, allocation deltas, and calculation traces — not a generated rationalization. Combined with ISO/IEC 42001 certification, AI outputs inherit the same audit defense as the rest of the Zuora ledger.

### CMS integration

Each landing page section maps to one CMS entry of type `LandingSection`, with fields:

- `headline` · `subhead` · `body` · `cta` · `proof_slot` · `target_keyword` · `pillar_id` · `personalization_tag`

**Reusable records (referenced by ID, not copied):**

- `ProofPoint` — Harris Poll stats, customer quotes, certifications. Updating a stat in one place propagates to every section that uses it.
- `CTA` — button labels and destinations. Changing "Watch a demo" once updates every section.
- `Persona` — segment tags for future personalization.
